Careers & Internships

Join an elite team of legal practitioners and shape the future of trial and appellate advocacy in Bangalore.

"Siji Malayil Associates offers an intensive, hands-on environment for legal professionals and scholars who are dedicated to rigorous trial preparation, deep research, and elite courtroom representation."

At Siji Malayil Associates, we believe that the best legal strategies are forged through absolute preparation, scientific scrutiny of evidence, and an unyielding commitment to our clients' rights. Located in the heart of Bengaluru's commercial district, Manipal Centre, we operate at the forefront of criminal trial defense, civil litigation, PMLA inquiries, service matters, and commercial dispute resolution.

We are always looking to welcome bright, driven, and ethical minds into our chambers—whether as long-term associates or as part of our rigorous trial-court internship programs. If you possess a sharp analytical mind, a strong work ethic, and an eagerness to confront complex legal challenges in the courtroom, we invite you to build your career with us.

Litigation Internship Program

Our Internship Program is designed strictly for proactive law students (enrolled in recognized 3-year or 5-year LL.B. courses) who are genuinely interested in litigation and trial practice. We do not offer passive internships; our interns are fully integrated into daily chamber activities and actively shadow our senior litigators in the courts.

  • Active Courtroom Exposure: Shadow advocates in the High Court of Karnataka, City Civil Courts, Sessions Courts, Magistrate Courts, and various tribunals across Bangalore to understand live trial proceedings and cross-examinations.
  • Case Analysis & Briefing: Participate in detailed file read-throughs, map prosecutorial narratives, extract facts, and prepare comprehensive case briefs for active trials.
  • Advanced Legal Research: Draft detailed search memorandums on intricate questions of criminal law, NDPS procedures, constitutional writs, and civil litigation using SCC Online and raw case registries.
  • Drafting Experience: Assist in the preparation and review of bail applications, writ petitions, civil pleadings, commercial contracts, and title scrutiny reports.

Selection Criteria & Expectations

  • Minimum Duration: A minimum commitment of 4 consecutive weeks is required for all interns to ensure a comprehensive, meaningful learning cycle.
  • Academic Standing & Research: Excellent academic performance, with highly demonstrated skills in drafting and digital legal databases.
  • Professionalism: Strong adherence to the bar's ethical boundaries, absolute confidentiality regarding case documents, and absolute punctuality in court attendance.

Associate Positions

For qualified advocates looking to represent clients in high-stakes litigation, SMA offers a structured career path with immense trial exposure. Associates at our firm are trained to handle drafting, police liaisoning, regulatory responses (such as ED/PMLA summons), client briefing, and direct arguments in court under the mentorship of our Managing Partner, Adv. Siji Malayil.
