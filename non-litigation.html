Non-Litigation Advisory & Conveyancing

Diligent real estate title search, detailed contract drafting, and strategic risk advisory.

"Preventing legal risks before they emerge through rigorous title due diligence, precise drafting of contracts, and sound corporate compliance strategies."

A solid legal foundation prevents the cost and distraction of future courtroom litigation. Siji Malayil Associates provides highly meticulous non-litigation advisory and conveyancing services, specializing in property title due diligence for home buyers and real estate developers, drafting robust commercial contracts, and structured inheritance planning.

In Bangalore's complex real estate market, our dedicated property desk conducts rigorous title searches spanning 30+ years, checking government records, land maps, and tax documents to guarantee absolute security for your investments. We also draft and negotiate highly protective Joint Development Agreements (JDA), Sale Deeds, Lease Agreements, Wills, and Corporate Partnerships, ensuring complete safety from the very beginning.

Key Areas of Focus

  • 30+ Years Property Title Scrutiny & Due Diligence
  • Drafting Joint Development Agreements (JDA) & Sale Deeds
  • Commercial Contract Drafting, Negotiation & Review
  • Estate Planning: Drafting Wills, Codicils, & Gift Deeds
  • RERA Registration & Legal Compliance Audits
  • Corporate Formation, Partnerships, & Legal Advisory

Successful non-litigation work is defined by meticulous attention to detail, proactive risk identification, and clean drafting that removes all potential ambiguities from your contracts and properties.
